My reflection

Building this website was quite challenging for me, as not only was I falling a little bit behind on class, but It also took me a long time to figure out my website design. Throughout most of the lessons, I spend trying to learn more techniques and grasp a better general understanding of how to code in general, causing not much work to be done until the last moment. I have learnt so many new things, not just on how to code, but how to manage my time and understand coding better. Some especially hard things that took me an extremely long time to understand and debug, such as the slideshow of pictures. Not only was adding the table and column concepts quite hard to learn, but it would end up not working a lot of the time, especially when I added my images. The table would often not render properly, be too small or too big. I had to solve this issue by either adding more text in my html or changing the margin or table size in my css. Another time, the image slide show was to left and was cut off even when it was supposed to be in the middle of the table. I had to fix this by changing the position from relative to absolute and adding the code 'object-fit: contain;' The concepts of position, fading in and fading out were very hard to understand, especially on code. I am grateful that I had my mums help throughout this difficult process, she went through my code and helped me debug mistakes that had me stumped for a long period of time. I have also learnt how to move items around better, using flexbox or other methods, which will be extremely useful to my later years.
